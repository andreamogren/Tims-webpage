About

Are you drowning in notifications and a fruitless pursuit of likes? We have the solution for you! Escape the digital reality for few hours at Off the grid, a bar with cell-phone jammers. There is a landline, you know, one of those old-fashioned phones stuck to the wall that died with the Y2K. Tim and Sean promise to whip out a night you'll never forget. Pants optional.
